To view our project ideas, or to propose a project idea, open an issue at github.com/open-austin/project-ideas. If you’re not familiar with GitHub, feel free to contact us instead. These projects have been produced by Open Austin contributors.

Project Status

The project status indicates the maturity and usability of a project. Only beta and deployed projects should be considered ready for general public use. The project status values are:

  • deployed – provided for public use, may be undergoing further development, feedback and bug reports invited
  • beta – provided for use and feedback, not complete, still in development
  • in development – provided for development use, not fully functional, not intended for public use
  • ideation – proof-of-concept prototype, not robust or fully functional
  • archival – of historical interest, not being actively developed, feedback not invited, may need developer support
