Date: Mon, 4 Feb 2013
Time: 6:45 - 9:00 PM (link)
Location: HubAustin Coworking, 706B W Ben White Blvd, Suite 200, Austin, TX 78704 (link)

On Monday evening, February 4, Open Austin will be conducting an ATX OpenGov Hack Night.

OpenGov Hack Night is for developers and designers to meet and work on projects in support of civic applications and open data. You can bring a project to work on, get help with a project you are working on, solicit volunteers to help you with a project you are working on, or just be there to help.

Space is limited. If you wish to attend, please RSVP as soon as possible. Pizza and drinks will be provided for those who RSVP.

RSVP here: https://openaustin.ticketbud.com/hack-night-20130204

Please be on time. Doors will be open 6:45 - 7:00. At 7:00 we close the doors and begin hacking. If you need to be let in after that time please call or text Chip Rosenthal at 512-573-5174.

Additional information, including project proposals, here: http://tinyurl.com/b9zq4gn
